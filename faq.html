    KEY INFO + FAQ

    KEY INFO

    We're dedicated to providing you with the best festival experience possible. We've put together all the info you need so you're well informed. If you still need anything, please get in touch!
    TICKETS/RESALES

    Tickets are purchased through the Eventbrite website using the 'Tickets' link.

    We will scan your Eventbrite ticket displayed from your phone, make sure you have it ready with enough battery charge. If you don't have a phone capable of this, please print for scanning.

    All resales must be made here through Tixel. Beware of scammers selling fake tickets. Using Tixel reduces the risk of being scammed as they reissue the ticket to your name after you buy and cancel the old ticket.

    Public Ferry/Charter Bus tickets are available here from Fullers.

    TRANSPORT

    Waiheke Island is a 40-minute passenger ferry ride from Auckland Downtown Ferry Terminal via Fullers or 1-hour car ferry ride from Half Moon Bay/Wynyard Wharf via Sealink. The festival is a 25 minute drive from the Matiatia ferry terminal.

    The festival is approximately 15 minutes drive from Kennedy Point ferry terminal and 25 minutes from Matiatia Ferry terminal.

    For public transport, catch the #2 bus to Rocky Bay and ask the driver for Rangihoua Estate, 1 Gordons Road. Or you could cycle, walk or catch a taxi. Plan your journey on the Auckland Transport website.

    STAYING SAFE

    DISCO DISCO is a safe, inclusive space for all. If anyone is making you feel uncomfortable, please text 'HELP' and your situation to 2446 (active the entire weekend) or talk to security (in yellow high vis jackets) or one of our staff. Look out for others. Be kind.

    START/FINISH TIMES

    Gates open at 2pm and the event finishes at midnight on both Sat Jan 23rd and Sun Jan 24th

    Hold tight - Set times and the offial program will be posted soon

    SHOULD I DRESS UP?

    Of course! We make an effort to create a magical site and encourage everyone to add to this by getting creative and colourful in your dress. DISCO DISCO is a lush, vibrant, dancing space in an island olive grove. Be part of the magic. We'll reward your efforts with spot prizes on the night.

    NON-ALCOHOLIC DRINKS/FOOD

    There is free water available on-site and a range of delicious non-alcoholic drinks and low-alcohol beers. You can bring empty water bottles on site. It's a long day in the sun, so please pace yourself. There is of course our amazing variety of food partners onsite with vegatarien, vegan and gluten free options.

    SMOKING

    Since there is a high fire risk on the island. Smoking is not allowed in the forest or under covered areas. There will be signs there to help you out. Please be resposnsible.

    RECYCLING

    We are working towards a zero-waste event with Waiheke Resources Trust. Please help us by using the appropriate bin for recyclables and compostables, they will be super easy to identify.
